My Journey into Web Development

Posted on March 15, 2025 by Violet Talemwa

My journey into web development started with curiosity and a lot of Google searches. I had always loved problem-solving and creative design, but I didn’t know how they could come together — until I discovered HTML and CSS.

The early days were rough. I remember spending hours trying to center a div and wondering why my layout didn’t look like the tutorial. But those struggles made every success feel that much more rewarding.

My First Wins

Creating my first functional website — even if it looked basic — was a turning point. I learned how to structure content, style it with CSS, and even add some interactivity using JavaScript.

What Kept Me Going

Community support, online tutorials, and my growing confidence fueled my passion. Over time, I started building real-world projects and learning more about responsive design and accessibility.

Today, I’m working on more advanced projects, including full-stack apps, and mentoring others just starting their own coding journeys.
