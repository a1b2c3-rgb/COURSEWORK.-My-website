My Journey into Web Development

Posted on March 15, 2025 by Violet Talemwa

My interest in web development began with a lot of Google searches and curiosity.
Up until I learned about HTML and CSS, I had always loved solving problems and designing creatively,
but I had no idea how they might coexist.

The initial days were difficult.
I can still recall spending hours attempting to center a div and
wondering why my layout didn't resemble the one in the instruction.
However, such hardships added to the satisfaction of every victory.

My First Wins

Even though it was simple, making my first working website was a turning point in my life. I gained knowledge about content organization, CSS styling, and JavaScript interactivity.

What Kept Me Going

My interest was fueled by online tutorials, community support, and my developing self-confidence.
I gradually began working on actual projects and gained more knowledge about accessibility and responsive design.

I am currently working on more complex projects, such as full-stack apps,
and coaching those who are just beginning their own coding careers.
